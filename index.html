Agentic Deep Research Agent

AI Agents • RAG • Research
Developed an end-to-end autonomous deep research agent that performs iterative literature analysis over a corpus of 568 LLM-agent research papers. Implemented a modular agent architecture consisting of query decomposition, hybrid retrieval (vector search + BM25), reciprocal rank fusion, cross-encoder reranking, reflection-based iterative search, grounded answer synthesis, and citation verification to generate evidence-backed responses for complex research queries.

Built a scalable corpus generation pipeline featuring automated arXiv collection, resilient rate-limit handling, PDF extraction, semantic chunking, embedding generation, and persistent vector indexing, while expanding corpus coverage through targeted benchmark retrieval. Evaluated multiple ablated agent variants to quantify the impact of planning, retrieval, reranking, reflection, and verification on faithfulness, citation precision, latency, and overall research quality.
